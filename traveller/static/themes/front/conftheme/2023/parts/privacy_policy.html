Methods of Storage and Types of Data Collected

Website Forms

We use forms with input fields to collect data, these input fields specify the type of data that you are entering. Hidden input fields may be used by the website in order to tell what form was complete and sent.

Server Logs

Logs may be taken by our infrastructure and website of your connection. These logs will contain, but is not limited to your IP address and the time that you visited a page. This can be extended to include the browser you were using at the time, and also the device you were using.

Secure Session Cookies

We use secure session cookies to store information on your device that allows our website to uniquely identify you from other visitors. The secure session cookie file(s) are encrypted and used as a method of storing temporary information that is relevant to only your usage of our website (such as, but not limited to: Information to identify if you are logged in or not, temporary data storage between website pages and search box settings)

The secure cookie file(s) is(are) essential in the working of our website and will expire in 8 hours, this time will renew if you visit again within 8 hours. The expiration time of the cookie file is subject to change at any time and by using this website you agree to allow us to store this temporary data on your device.

Tracking Cookies

Tracking cookies may be used on our website and will only be activated with your consent. These tracking cookies are used to store information to uniquely identify you across our website and or any external website, how much time you remained on one page, and details about your device.
